How to play Gfight?

Rule 1

The game is played by up to 50 players who are dropped on an island and have to fight to survive until the end.

Rule 2

The objective of the game is to be the last player or team standing.

Rule 3

Players can choose their landing spot and search for weapons, ammunition, and other resources to survive and fight against other players.

Rule 4

The game is played in rounds, and each round lasts for approximately 10 minutes.
